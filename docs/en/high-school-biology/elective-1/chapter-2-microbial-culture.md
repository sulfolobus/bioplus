## Knowledge Checklist

## Typical Question

Question: Colonies are too dense to count after plating. What should be improved?

How to solve: Increase dilution level and set parallel replicates.

## Common Pitfalls

- Confusing disinfection and sterilization
- Errors in dilution-factor calculation
- Non-sterile handling sequence
