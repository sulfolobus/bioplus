# Chapter 3: Plant Tissue Culture

## Knowledge Checklist

- Cell totipotency in plants
- Explant preparation and aseptic inoculation
- Dedifferentiation and redifferentiation
- Hormone ratio effects

## Typical Question

Question: Why might callus form but fail to regenerate organs?

How to solve: Check hormone ratio, media composition, light, and temperature.

## Common Pitfalls

- Overgeneralizing totipotency without conditions
- Ignoring sterile operation details
- Mixing up dedifferentiation and redifferentiation
