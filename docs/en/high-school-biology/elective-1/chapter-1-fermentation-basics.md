# Chapter 1: Fermentation Basics

## Knowledge Checklist

- Microbial basis of fermentation
- Aerobic and anaerobic fermentation conditions

## Typical Question

Question: Why can fermentation yield change at different temperatures?

How to solve: Link microbial growth, enzyme activity, and contamination risk.

## Common Pitfalls

- Defining fermentation as strictly anaerobic
- Ignoring sterilization and sealing
- Tracking only final product without process data
