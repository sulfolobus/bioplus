# Chapter 5: Energy Supply and Use

## Knowledge Checklist

- Enzyme action and properties
- ATP structure and role
- Aerobic vs anaerobic respiration
- Light reactions and carbon fixation in photosynthesis

## Typical Question

Question: If light intensity increases, will photosynthetic rate always keep rising?

How to solve: Analyze limiting factors such as CO2, temperature, and enzyme activity.

## Common Pitfalls

- Thinking enzymes are consumed in reactions
- Treating ATP as long-term energy storage
- Memorizing equations without process understanding
