# Chapter 4: Transport Across the Membrane

## Knowledge Checklist

- Diffusion, facilitated diffusion, and active transport
- Osmosis and plasmolysis
- Endocytosis and exocytosis
- Factors affecting transport rate

## Typical Question

Question: What happens to a plant cell in a hypertonic sucrose solution, and why?

How to solve: Use water potential difference and membrane permeability to explain.

## Common Pitfalls

- Assuming active transport needs no carrier proteins
- Treating concentration gradient as the only factor
- Equating plasmolysis with cell death
