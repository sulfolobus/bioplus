# Chapter 1: Introduction to Cells

## Knowledge Checklist

- Core ideas of cell theory
- Differences between prokaryotic and eukaryotic cells
- Basic microscope operation
- Levels of biological organization

## Typical Question

Question: A cell has ribosomes and a cell membrane but no nucleus enclosed by a nuclear membrane. What type of cell is it?

How to solve: Use key structural evidence first, then eliminate impossible groups.

## Common Pitfalls

- Treating “no nucleus” as “no genetic material”
- Classifying viruses as prokaryotic cells
- Mixing up image direction under a microscope
