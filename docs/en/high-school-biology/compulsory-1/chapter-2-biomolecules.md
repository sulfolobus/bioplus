# Chapter 2: Biomolecules in Cells

## Knowledge Checklist

- Major elements and compounds in living systems
- Structure and function of carbohydrates, lipids, proteins, and nucleic acids
- Roles of water and inorganic salts
- Principles of basic biochemical tests

## Typical Question

Question: Why are proteins considered the major performers of life activities?

How to solve: Connect structural diversity to functional diversity with examples.

## Common Pitfalls

- Treating nucleic acids as DNA only
- Confusing test methods for reducing sugar and lipid
- Making absolute claims about lipid solubility
