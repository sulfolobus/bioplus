# Chapter 3: Basic Cell Structure

## Knowledge Checklist

- Cell membrane structure and functions
- Organelles and their functional roles
- Coordination within the endomembrane system
- Nucleus structure and genetic control

## Typical Question

Question: Describe the pathway of a secreted protein from synthesis to export.

How to solve: Reconstruct the sequence of organelles and explain each step briefly.

## Common Pitfalls

- Calling ribosomes membrane-bound organelles
- Mixing up mitochondria and chloroplast roles
- Treating the cell membrane as a static layer

## Self-check Quiz

1. Why is the membrane called a system boundary?
2. What are the major functions of lysosomes?
3. How does the nucleus affect protein synthesis?
