# Chapter 6: Cell Life Cycle

## Knowledge Checklist

- Mitosis stages and chromosome behavior
- Cell differentiation, aging, and apoptosis
- Basic features of cancer cells
- Concept of cell totipotency

## Typical Question

Question: Chromosomes align at the equatorial plane. Which mitotic stage is most likely?

How to solve: Identify chromosome arrangement and separation status.

## Common Pitfalls

- Confusing apoptosis with necrosis
- Claiming differentiation changes genes themselves
- Misreading mitosis diagrams
