# Chapter 2: Bioethics and Regulation

## Knowledge Checklist

- Core principles of bioethics
- Informed consent and privacy
- Ethical boundaries in human research
- Biosafety-related regulation

## Typical Question

Question: Why is informed consent non-negotiable in biomedical research?

How to solve: Explain participant rights, risk disclosure, and legal legitimacy.

## Common Pitfalls

- Judging ethics by effectiveness alone
- Treating regulation as anti-science by default
- Ignoring participant protection

## Self-check Quiz

1. How is the principle of respect applied in practice?
2. Why should genetic data be strictly protected?
3. How can research freedom and ethics review coexist?
