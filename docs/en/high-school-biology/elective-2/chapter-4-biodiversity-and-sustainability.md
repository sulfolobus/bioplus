# Chapter 4: Biodiversity and Sustainability

## Knowledge Checklist

- Levels of biodiversity
- Ecosystem services
- Drivers of biodiversity loss
- Conservation and sustainable use strategies

## Typical Question

Question: Bird species richness declines after wetland loss. Explain from an ecosystem perspective.

How to solve: Analyze habitat, food resources, migration routes, and network integrity.

## Common Pitfalls

- Tracking species count only, not ecosystem function
- Framing conservation vs development as absolute opposition
- Missing long-term monitoring perspective
