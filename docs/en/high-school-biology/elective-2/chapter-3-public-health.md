# Chapter 3: Public Health and Personal Health

## Knowledge Checklist

- Core links in infectious disease spread
- Vaccination and herd immunity
- Chronic disease prevention strategies
- Health behavior interventions

## Typical Question

Question: How can higher vaccination coverage indirectly protect unvaccinated people?

How to solve: Explain transmission interruption and reduction of effective reproduction number.

## Common Pitfalls

- Defining public health as hospital treatment only
- Assuming vaccine protection is always complete
- Underestimating behavior-based prevention
