# Chapter 5: Ecosystem and Stability

## Knowledge Checklist

- Components of ecosystems
- Food chains and food webs
- Energy flow and matter cycling
- Information transfer
- Resistance vs resilience stability

## Typical Question

Question: If top predators decline sharply, what cascading effects may occur?

How to solve: Analyze trophic links and feedback in the food web.

## Common Pitfalls

- Writing energy transfer efficiency as 100%
- Confusing one-way energy flow with cyclic matter flow
- Ignoring openness of ecosystems

## Self-check Quiz

1. Why are producers foundational in ecosystems?
2. Why is carbon cycling tied to human activities?
3. Can high resistance and high resilience always coexist?
