# Chapter 4: Population and Community

## Knowledge Checklist

- Population parameters and growth models
- Community structure and species interactions
- Primary vs secondary succession
- Basic niche concept

## Typical Question

Question: A population shows logistic (S-shaped) growth. What limits growth later?

How to solve: Discuss resources, space, predation, and carrying capacity.

## Common Pitfalls

- Mixing up J-shaped and S-shaped model conditions
- Misclassifying competition and predation
- Assuming succession direction is fixed forever
