# Chapter 3: Plant Hormone Regulation

## Knowledge Checklist

- Auxin transport and concentration effects
- Mechanisms of phototropism and apical dominance
- Roles of major plant hormones
- Synergy and antagonism among hormones

## Typical Question

Question: Why do lateral buds often grow faster after removing the shoot apex?

How to solve: Explain changes in auxin distribution and dominance release.

## Common Pitfalls

- Treating auxin effect as always stimulatory
- Not linking hormone action to concentration and tissue
- Missing control groups in experiments
