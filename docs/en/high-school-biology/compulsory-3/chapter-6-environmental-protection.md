# Chapter 6: Environmental Protection

## Knowledge Checklist

- Levels of biodiversity
- In-situ and ex-situ conservation
- Drivers of ecological degradation
- Sustainable development strategies

## Typical Question

Question: Why is in-situ conservation often prioritized over ex-situ conservation?

How to solve: Explain ecosystem integrity and long-term sustainability.

## Common Pitfalls

- Focusing on single species only
- Treating conservation and development as absolute opposites
- Ignoring long-term monitoring

## Self-check Quiz

1. What risks come from loss of genetic diversity?
2. Can in-situ and ex-situ conservation be combined?
3. Give one biology-based sustainability case.
