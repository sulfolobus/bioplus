# Chapter 6: Breeding and Genetic Engineering

## Knowledge Checklist

- Comparison of hybrid, mutation, and haploid breeding
- Core logic of transgenic technology
- Trait-targeted breeding design
- Value and limitations of modern breeding

## Typical Question

Question: For disease resistance, compare conventional breeding and transgenic breeding.

How to solve: Compare time, precision, scope, and social acceptance.

## Common Pitfalls

- Assuming genetic engineering replaces all classic breeding
- Ignoring the selection stage
- Confusing DNA transfer with protein transfer
