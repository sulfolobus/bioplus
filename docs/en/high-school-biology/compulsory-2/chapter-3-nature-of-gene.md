# Chapter 3: The Nature of the Gene

## Knowledge Checklist

- Evidence that DNA is the major genetic material
- Double-helix structure of DNA
- Semi-conservative DNA replication
- Base pairing and ratio calculations

## Typical Question

Question: If A is 30% in a DNA segment, what are T, G, and C?

How to solve: Use A=T, G=C, and total base percentage equals 100%.

## Common Pitfalls

- Writing DNA as the only genetic material
- Confusing replication with transcription
- Ignoring double-strand constraints in calculations
