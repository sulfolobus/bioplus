## Typical Question

Question: How can a codon mutation affect protein structure?

How to solve: Compare silent, missense, and nonsense outcomes.

## Common Pitfalls

- Writing ribosome as the transcription site
- Reversing codon/anticodon orientation
- Assuming one gene always determines one fixed trait
