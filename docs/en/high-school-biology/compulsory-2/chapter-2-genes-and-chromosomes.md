# Chapter 2: Genes and Chromosomes

## Knowledge Checklist

- Meiosis process and gamete formation
- Homologous chromosomes vs sister chromatids
- Sex-linked inheritance patterns
- Family pedigree analysis

## Typical Question

Question: A trait appears much more frequently in males. Which inheritance pattern is likely?

How to solve: Use sex distribution and generational transmission clues.

## Common Pitfalls

- Confusing homologous chromosomes with sister chromatids
- Assuming all sex-linked traits are X-linked recessive
- Misplacing key events across meiotic stages
