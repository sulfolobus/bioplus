# Chapter 5: Mutation and Variation

## Knowledge Checklist

- Gene mutation concepts and features
- Forms of genetic recombination
- Chromosome structure/number variations
- Heritable vs non-heritable variation

## Typical Question

Question: Compare an environmentally induced skin color change with a mutation-caused trait change.

How to solve: Judge whether DNA is altered and whether it can be inherited.

## Common Pitfalls

- Assuming all mutations are harmful
- Mixing up mutation and recombination
- Confusing gene-level and chromosome-level changes

## Self-check Quiz

1. Why are mutations raw material for evolution?
2. What is an advantage of polyploid breeding?
3. How do you identify heritable variation?
