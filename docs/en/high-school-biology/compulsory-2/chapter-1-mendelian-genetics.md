# Chapter 1: Mendelian Genetics

## Knowledge Checklist

- Mendel's experimental design logic
- Law of segregation and law of independent assortment
- Dominant/recessive traits and genotype terms
- Test cross strategy

## Typical Question

Question: Why does a monohybrid F2 often show a 3:1 phenotype ratio?

How to solve: Derive gametes and genotype ratio, then map to phenotype ratio.

## Common Pitfalls

- Equating genotype ratio with phenotype ratio
- Mixing up hybridization and test cross
- Probability errors in multi-step problems
