# Chapter 7: Modern Evolution Theory

## Knowledge Checklist

- Population as the unit of evolution
- Gene frequency and genotype frequency
- Natural selection and adaptation
- Isolation and speciation

## Typical Question

Question: Why can pesticide resistance increase after long-term pesticide use?

How to solve: Explain with variation, selection pressure, and frequency shift.

## Common Pitfalls

- Treating evolution as individual-level change
- Thinking selection creates mutations intentionally
- Ignoring environmental context

## Self-check Quiz

1. What is gene frequency?
2. Why is reproductive isolation key to speciation?
3. Why is evolution not simply “becoming better”?
