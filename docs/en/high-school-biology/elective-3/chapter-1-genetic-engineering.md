# Chapter 1: Genetic Engineering Basics

## Knowledge Checklist

- Target gene acquisition
- Vector structure and selection

## Typical Question

Question: Why are plasmids commonly used as vectors?

How to solve: Explain replication origin, marker genes, cloning sites, and operability.

## Common Pitfalls

- Mixing gene introduction with gene expression outcomes
- Misremembering enzyme recognition logic
- Ignoring vector screening design
