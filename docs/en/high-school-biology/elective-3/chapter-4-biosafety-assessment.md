# Chapter 4: Biosafety and Technology Assessment

## Knowledge Checklist

- Biosafety risk identification
- Assessment logic for genetically modified organisms
- Laboratory biosafety levels
- Risk communication principles

## Typical Question

Question: What dimensions should be assessed for a new GM crop?

How to solve: Cover environmental impact, food safety, ecological spread, and governance feasibility.

## Common Pitfalls

- Equating “unknown risk” with “high risk” directly
- Ignoring long-term monitoring
- Assessing without a baseline comparison
