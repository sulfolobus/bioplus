# Hearing Loss Treatment

Hearing loss is a common sensory dysfunction. This article introduces treatment strategies.

## Types of Hearing Loss

| Type | Cause | Treatment |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Conductive | Outer/middle ear disease | Surgery/hearing aids |
| Sensorineural | Inner ear/auditory nerve | Hearing aids/cochlear implants |
| Mixed | Both | Comprehensive treatment |

## Treatment Strategies

### Pharmacotherapy

- Glucocorticoids (sudden hearing loss)
- Vasodilators
- Neurotrophic drugs

### Device Therapy

| Device | Applicable Situation |
|--------|---------------------|
| Hearing aids | Mild to moderate hearing loss |
| Cochlear implants | Severe to profound sensorineural hearing loss |
| Bone conduction hearing aids | Conductive hearing loss |
