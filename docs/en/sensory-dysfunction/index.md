# Sensory Dysfunction

Sensory dysfunction involves loss or impairment of vision, hearing, smell, taste, and touch.

## Main Types

### Blindness

Partial or complete loss of visual function, including total blindness and low vision.

### Hearing Loss

Decreased auditory function, from mild to severe.

### Olfactory Disorders

Reduced or lost sense of smell.

### Gustatory Disorders

Reduced or abnormal sense of taste.

## Research Directions

### Sensory Rehabilitation

- Artificial vision
- Cochlear implants
- Olfactory restoration

### Brain-Machine Interface

- Neuromodulation
- Sensory substitution
- Neural repair
