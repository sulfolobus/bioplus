# Animal Physiology

This topic focuses on how animals maintain homeostasis, move, sense, regulate, and defend themselves.

## Knowledge Checklist

- Nervous system structure and signal transmission
- Endocrine system and hormone feedback
- Coordination among circulatory, respiratory, digestive, and excretory systems
- Immune system components, responses, and memory
- Locomotion, sensory organs, and behavior regulation
- Thermoregulation, osmoregulation, and acid-base balance

## Typical Question

Question: Why do heart rate, breathing rate, and lactate level change after intense exercise?

How to solve: Connect energy demand, anaerobic metabolism, hormonal response, and compensation mechanisms.

## Common Pitfalls

- Separating neural and hormonal regulation too rigidly
- Ignoring the hierarchy from innate to adaptive immunity
- Treating hormones as if they directly change cell types
- Missing feedback loops in homeostasis problems
