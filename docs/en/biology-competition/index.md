# Biology Olympiad

This section is designed for learners preparing for biology competitions, with a focus on deeper topic review and advanced training.

## Competition Focus

- Cell biology
- Molecular genetics
- Plant and animal physiology
- Ecology and evolution
- Basic biochemistry
- Experimental design and data reading

## Training Advice

### 1. Review by topic

Instead of following the textbook only, build separate notes for cell biology, genetics, ecology, and lab work.

### 2. Strengthen information integration

Competition problems often combine concepts, charts, and experimental results, so practice extracting key information quickly.

### 3. Practice scientific communication

Beyond solving problems, train yourself to explain observations, state hypotheses, and justify conclusions clearly.
