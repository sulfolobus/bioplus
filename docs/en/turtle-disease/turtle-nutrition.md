# Turtle Nutrition Guide

## Basic Nutritional Requirements

### Protein

- Young turtles need higher protein (40-50%)
- Adult turtles need lower protein (15-25%)
- Sources: insects, fish/shrimp, turtle food

### Calcium

- Very important for shell health
- Sources: cuttlefish bone, calcium powder, shrimp shells
- Vitamin D3 needed for absorption

### Vitamins

| Vitamin | Function | Source |
|---------|----------|--------|
| Vitamin A | Skin, mucosal health | Carrots, liver |
| Vitamin D3 | Calcium absorption | Sunlight, fish liver oil |
| Vitamin E | Antioxidant | Plant oils |
| Vitamin B | Metabolism | Balanced diet |

## Food Types

### Aquatic Turtle Food

| Type | Ratio | Examples |
|------|-------|----------|
| Turtle pellets | 50% | Commercial food |
| Animal protein | 30% | Fish, insects |
| Plant matter | 20% | Aquatic plants, vegetables |

### Semi-aquatic Turtle Food

| Type | Ratio | Examples |
|------|-------|----------|
| Turtle pellets | 30% | Semi-aquatic food |
| Animal protein | 30% | Insects, snails |
| Plant matter | 40% | Fruits, vegetables |

### Terrestrial Turtle Food

| Type | Ratio | Examples |
|------|-------|----------|
| High-fiber vegetables | 70% | Grasses, leafy greens |
| Fruits | 20% | Berries, apple |
| Turtle pellets | 10% | Terrestrial food |

## Feeding Guide

### Feeding Frequency

| Age | Frequency |
|-----|-----------|
| Juvenile | 1-2 times daily |
| Adult | Once every 1-2 days |
| Before hibernation | Increase feeding |

### Feeding Amount

- About the size of the head
- Should eat within 10-15 minutes
- Avoid overfeeding

## Precautions

### Foods to Avoid

- Processed foods
- High salt/sugar foods
- Raw lean meat
- Dog/cat food
- Onions/garlic

### Nutritional Supplements

- Cuttlefish bone: calcium supplement
- Calcium powder: 1-2 times per week
- Vitamins: 1-2 times per month

### Common Problems

**Refusing Food Causes**:
- Temperature too low
- Environment not suitable
- Disease
- Breeding season

**Obesity**:
- Control feeding amount
- Increase exercise space
- Adjust diet composition
