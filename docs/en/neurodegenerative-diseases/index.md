# Neurodegenerative Diseases

Neurodegenerative diseases are conditions characterized by progressive loss of neuron structure or function, primarily affecting the central and peripheral nervous systems.

## Major Disease Types

### Alzheimer's Disease

The most common neurodegenerative disease, primarily manifested as memory decline and cognitive dysfunction.

### Parkinson's Disease

A neurodegenerative disease affecting motor control, with main symptoms including tremor, rigidity, and bradykinesia.

### Amnesia

Memory impairment caused by various factors, including aging, trauma, and disease.

## Research Directions

### Etiology Research

### Treatment Approaches

- Drug therapy
- Stem cell therapy
