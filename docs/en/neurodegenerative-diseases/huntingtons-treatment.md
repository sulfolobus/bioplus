# Huntington's Disease Treatment

Huntington's disease is an autosomal dominant neurodegenerative disorder.

## Disease Characteristics

### Causes

- HTT gene CAG repeat expansion
- Mutant huntingtin protein production
- Neurotoxicity

### Symptoms

| Symptom Type | Manifestations |
|-------------|----------------|
| Motor | Involuntary choreatic movements |
| Cognitive | Memory decline |
| Psychiatric | Depression, anxiety |

## Treatment Progress

### Symptomatic Treatment

- Tetrabenazine (chorea)
- Antipsychotic drugs

### New Therapies

- Gene editing therapy
- RNAi treatment
- Small molecule inhibitors
