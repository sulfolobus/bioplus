# Alzheimer's Disease Research Progress

Alzheimer's Disease (AD) is the most common neurodegenerative disease, accounting for 60-70% of all dementia cases.

## Disease Characteristics

### Pathological Features

- **Amyloid-beta Plaques**: Abnormal protein deposits outside cells
- **Tau Protein Neurofibrillary Tangles**: Abnormal protein structures inside neurons
- **Neuronal Death**: Especially in the hippocampus and cortex

### Symptoms

| Stage | Symptoms |
|-------|----------|
| Early | Memory decline, especially recent memory; word-finding difficulty |
| Middle | Cognitive decline, disorientation, behavioral changes |
| Late | Severe memory loss, loss of language ability, inability to care for self |

## Pathogenesis

### Main Theories

1. **Amyloid Hypothesis**
   - Overproduction or inadequate clearance of Aβ protein
   - Leads to neurotoxicity

2. **Tau Hypothesis**
   - Hyperphosphorylation of tau protein
   - Leads to neurofibrillary tangles

3. **Neuroinflammation Hypothesis**
   - Chronic neuroinflammation accelerates disease progression

### Risk Factors

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Age | Largest risk factor, significantly increases after 65 |
| Genetics | APP, PSEN1, PSEN2 gene mutations |
| Lifestyle | Lack of exercise, poor diet, smoking |
| Cardiovascular | Hypertension, diabetes, high cholesterol |

## Diagnosis

### Clinical Diagnosis

- Cognitive assessment (MMSE, MoCA)
- Medical history
- Neurological examination

### Biomarkers

| Biomarker | Method | Significance |
|-----------|--------|--------------|
| Aβ42 | CSF testing | Decreased suggests AD |
| Tau protein | CSF testing | Elevated suggests AD |
| PET scan | Amyloid imaging | Plaque deposition |

## Treatment Progress

### Approved Drugs

| Drug | Type | Function |
|------|------|----------|
| Memantine | NMDA antagonist | Improve cognition |
| Donepezil | Cholinesterase inhibitor | Symptom improvement |
| Aducanumab | Anti-Aβ antibody | Clear plaques (controversial) |
| Lecanemab | Anti-Aβ antibody | Slow progression |
| Donanemab | Anti-Aβ antibody | Clear plaques |

### Investigational Therapies

#### Immunotherapy

- Active immunization vaccines
- Monoclonal antibodies
- Nanobodies

#### Gene Therapy

- APOE gene editing
- Nerve growth factor gene therapy

#### Stem Cell Therapy

- Embryonic stem cell-derived neurons
- Induced pluripotent stem cells
- Neural stem cell transplantation

### Non-pharmacological Interventions

- Cognitive training
- Exercise therapy
- Music therapy
- Virtual reality training

## Prevention Strategies

### Modifiable Risk Factors

| Risk Factor | Prevention |
|-------------|------------|
| Lack of exercise | 150 minutes moderate exercise weekly |
| Smoking | Quit smoking |
| Obesity | Maintain healthy weight |
| Hypertension | Control blood pressure |
| Diabetes | Control blood sugar |
| Depression | Treat promptly |

### Protective Factors

- Higher education
- Social activities
- Cognitive stimulation
- Mediterranean diet
- Moderate alcohol consumption

- Microbiome and AD research
- AI in diagnosis
