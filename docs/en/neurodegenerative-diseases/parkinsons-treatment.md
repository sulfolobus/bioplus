# Parkinson's Disease Treatment

Parkinson's disease is a common neurodegenerative disorder. This article introduces its treatment progress.

## Disease Characteristics

### Pathological Features

- Loss of dopaminergic neurons in the substantia nigra
- Lewy body formation
- Lewy body deposition

### Symptoms

| Motor Symptoms | Non-Motor Symptoms |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Resting tremor | Hyposmia |
| Muscle rigidity | Sleep disorders |
| Bradykinesia | Constipation |
| Postural instability | Depression |

## Treatment Strategies

### Pharmacotherapy

| Drug Type | Representative Drugs |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Levodopa | Madopar |
| Dopamine agonists | Pramipexole |
| MAO-B inhibitors | Rasagiline |

### Surgical Treatment

- DBS (Deep Brain Stimulation)
- Nucleus lesioning

## New Therapy Progress

- Gene therapy
- Stem cell transplantation
- Immunotherapy
