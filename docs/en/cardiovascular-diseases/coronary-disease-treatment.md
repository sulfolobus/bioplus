# Coronary Heart Disease Treatment

Coronary heart disease is a major cause of death. This article introduces treatment strategies.

## Disease Characteristics

### Causes

- Coronary atherosclerosis
- Vascular stenosis or occlusion
- Myocardial ischemia

### Classification

| Type | Characteristics |
|------|-----------------|
| Stable angina | Stable symptoms |
| Unstable angina | Worsening symptoms |
| Myocardial infarction | Acute coronary syndrome |

## Treatment Strategies

### Pharmacotherapy

| Drug Type | Representative Drugs |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Antiplatelet | Aspirin |
| Statins | Atorvastatin |
| Beta-blockers | Metoprolol |
| ACEI/ARB | Benazepril |

### Interventional Therapy

- PCI (Percutaneous Coronary Intervention)
- Coronary stent implantation

### Surgical Treatment

- CABG (Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting)
