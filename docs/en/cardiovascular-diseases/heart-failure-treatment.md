# Heart Failure Treatment

Heart failure is the end stage of various heart diseases. This article introduces treatment progress.

## Disease Characteristics

### Causes

| Type | Common Causes |
|------|---------------|
| Systolic heart failure | Coronary heart disease, cardiomyopathy |
| Diastolic heart failure | Hypertension, elderly |

### Staging

| Stage | Characteristics |
|-------|----------------|
| A | Risk factors |
| B | Structural changes |
| C | Symptomatic |
| D | Refractory heart failure |

## Treatment Strategies

### Pharmacotherapy

| Drug Type | Representative Drugs |
|-----------|---------------------|
| ACEI/ARB | Benazepril |
| Beta-blockers | Metoprolol |
| Aldosterone antagonists | Spironolactone |
| SGLT2 inhibitors | Dapagliflozin |

### Device Therapy

- ICD (Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator)
- CRT (Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy)

### Surgical Treatment

- Heart transplantation
- Artificial heart
