# Cardiovascular Diseases

Cardiovascular diseases are conditions affecting the heart and blood vessel systems, and are one of the leading causes of death worldwide.

## Main Types

### Coronary Heart Disease

Coronary atherosclerosis causing myocardial ischemia and hypoxia.

### Hypertension

Persistently elevated blood pressure, a major risk factor for cardiovascular diseases.

### Heart Failure

Declining heart pumping function, unable to meet body's needs.

### Arrhythmias

Abnormal heart electrical activity, causing heartbeats that are too fast, too slow, or irregular.

### Cerebrovascular Diseases

Including ischemic and hemorrhagic stroke.

## Common Risk Factors

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Hypertension | Largest risk factor |
| High Blood Lipids | Atherosclerosis |
| Diabetes | Vascular damage |
| Smoking | Endothelial damage |
| Obesity | Metabolic syndrome |
| Lack of exercise | Cardiovascular function decline |
| Poor diet | Metabolic abnormalities |
