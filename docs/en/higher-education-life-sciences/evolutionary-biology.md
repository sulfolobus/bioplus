# Evolutionary Biology

## Position in the Curriculum

Explains biodiversity and adaptation, connecting ecology with genetic mechanisms.

## Chapter Structure and Checklist

1. Darwinian and modern synthesis frameworks
2. Population genetics and Hardy-Weinberg
3. Selection, drift, and gene flow
4. Speciation and phylogeny
5. Molecular evolution and common ancestry
