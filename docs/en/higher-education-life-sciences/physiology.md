# Physiology

## Position in the Curriculum

Focuses on functional regulation in organisms and organ systems.

## Chapter Structure and Checklist

1. Homeostasis and control loops
2. Neural and endocrine regulation
3. Circulatory and respiratory physiology
4. Digestive, renal, and metabolic physiology
5. System integration and disease phenotypes
