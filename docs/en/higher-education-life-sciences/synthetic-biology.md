# Synthetic Biology

## Position in the Curriculum

Introduces engineering design of biological systems for programmable functions.

## Chapter Structure and Checklist

1. Standard biological parts and design rules
2. Genetic circuits and control logic
3. Metabolic engineering and chassis design
4. Design-Build-Test-Learn cycle
5. Biosafety, ethics, and governance
