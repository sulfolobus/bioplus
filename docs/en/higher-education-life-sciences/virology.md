# Virology

## Position in the Curriculum

Focuses on viral structure, replication, evolution, and host interaction across basic biology, immunology, and public health.

## Chapter Structure and Checklist

1. Virus taxonomy and naming systems
2. Virion structure and genome organization
3. Viral replication cycles and host entry
4. Viral evolution, mutation, and host jump
5. Virology diagnostics, vaccines, and antiviral strategies
