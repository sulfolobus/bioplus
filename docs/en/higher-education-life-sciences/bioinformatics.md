# Bioinformatics

## Position in the Curriculum

Applies computation and statistics to sequence, genome, and omics data.

## Chapter Structure and Checklist

1. Databases and data standards
2. Sequence alignment and annotation
3. Genomics variant workflows
4. Transcriptomics and proteomics pipelines
5. Reproducible analysis and visualization
