# Structural Biology

## Position in the Curriculum

Uses molecular structure to explain function, dynamics, and molecular recognition.

## Chapter Structure and Checklist

1. Protein and nucleic acid structural hierarchy
2. X-ray crystallography fundamentals
3. NMR and cryo-EM methods
4. Complex formation and interaction interfaces
5. Structure-based drug discovery
