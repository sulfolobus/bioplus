# Biochemistry

## Position in the Curriculum

Focuses on biomolecular structure, enzymatic reactions, and metabolic regulation.

## Chapter Structure and Checklist

1. Biomolecules and structural principles
2. Enzyme kinetics and catalysis
3. Metabolism and pathway integration
4. Bioenergetics and ATP coupling
5. Biochemical basis of disease
