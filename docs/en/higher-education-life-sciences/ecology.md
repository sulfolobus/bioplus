# Ecology

## Position in the Curriculum

Studies organism-environment interactions from population to ecosystem scales.

## Chapter Structure and Checklist

1. Niche and organismal ecology
2. Population dynamics and life-history strategies
3. Community interactions and stability
4. Energy flow and biogeochemical cycles
5. Global change and biodiversity conservation
