# Developmental Biology

## Position in the Curriculum

Studies temporal and spatial programs from fertilization to mature organisms.

## Chapter Structure and Checklist

1. Gametogenesis and fertilization
2. Early embryogenesis and germ layers
3. Axis patterning and morphogenesis
4. Organogenesis and cell fate decisions
5. Stem cells, regeneration, and defects
