# Molecular Biology

## Position in the Curriculum

Explains molecular information flow and regulatory logic in living systems.

## Chapter Structure and Checklist

1. DNA replication and repair
2. Transcription and gene regulation
3. Translation and protein maturation
4. Epigenetics and non-coding RNA
5. Molecular methods and perturbation tools
