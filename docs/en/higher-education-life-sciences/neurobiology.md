# Neurobiology

## Position in the Curriculum

Explains how nervous systems process information from molecular to behavioral levels.

## Chapter Structure and Checklist

1. Neurons, glia, and excitability
2. Synaptic transmission and plasticity
3. Sensory and motor systems
4. Learning, memory, and behavior
5. Neurodegeneration and repair
