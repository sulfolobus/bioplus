# Genetics

## Position in the Curriculum

Studies inheritance and variation from classical genetics to genomic medicine.

## Chapter Structure and Checklist

1. Mendelian and post-Mendelian inheritance
2. Linkage, recombination, and mapping
3. Chromosomes and genome organization
4. Quantitative and population genetics
5. Human genetics and complex traits
