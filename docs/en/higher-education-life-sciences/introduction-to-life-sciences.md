# Introduction to Life Sciences

## Position in the Curriculum

This subject builds a whole-picture framework across levels of life, from ecosystems to molecules.

## Chapter Structure and Checklist

1. Levels of organization in life sciences
2. Scientific method and experimental logic
3. History of biological ideas and methods
4. Frontier directions: omics, AI, and biomanufacturing
