# Cell Biology

## Position in the Curriculum

Uses cells as the core unit to explain biological structure, function, and regulation.

## Chapter Structure and Checklist

1. Cell architecture and membrane systems
2. Cytoskeleton and intracellular transport
3. Cell cycle, death, and quality control
4. Signaling networks
5. Differentiation, senescence, and cancer
