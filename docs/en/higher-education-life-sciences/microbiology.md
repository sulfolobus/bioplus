# Microbiology

## Position in the Curriculum

Covers microbes across structure, metabolism, genetics, ecology, and application.

## Chapter Structure and Checklist

1. Microbial classification and identification
2. Growth, metabolism, and cultivation
3. Microbial genetics and resistance
4. Microbial ecology and microbiomes
5. Medical and industrial microbiology
