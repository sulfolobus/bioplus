# AI-Assisted Diagnosis

## Application of Artificial Intelligence in Medical Diagnosis

Artificial intelligence (AI) is revolutionizing the field of medical diagnosis. Through deep learning, machine learning, and other technologies, AI systems can play important roles in medical image analysis, pathological diagnosis, and decision support.

## Main Applications of AI Diagnosis

### 1. Medical Image Analysis

AI has achieved remarkable results in the field of medical imaging:

| Application Area | Typical Task | Accuracy |
|------------------|---------------|----------|
| Fundus Images | Diabetic Retinopathy Screening | 95%+ |
| Chest X-ray | Lung Nodule Detection | 90%+ |
| Skin Images | Skin Cancer Recognition | 95%+ |
| Pathology Slides | Cancer Cell Detection | Near expert level |

### 2. Pathological Diagnosis

- Digital pathology slide analysis
- Automatic cancer cell recognition and grading
- Immunohistochemistry result analysis

### 3. Clinical Decision Support

- Assisted diagnostic recommendations
- Treatment plan recommendations
- Risk prediction models

## Typical Application Scenarios

### Fundus AI

Diabetic retinopathy screening systems have been deployed in multiple hospitals, achieving automated screening.

### Lung AI

Automatic lung nodule detection systems can assist doctors in detecting early-stage lung cancer.

### Skin AI

Skin lesion identification systems can preliminarily determine whether it is malignant.

## Technical Advantages

1. **Efficiency Improvement**: Rapid processing of large volumes of images
2. **Consistency**: Reducing human error
3. **Scalability**: Easy to promote to primary care settings

## Challenges and Prospects

- **Data Privacy**: Medical data security
- **Regulatory Approval**: AI medical device approval
- **Human-Machine Collaboration**: Cooperation between AI and doctors
