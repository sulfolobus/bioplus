# Genetic Testing

Genetic testing is an important component of preventive medicine, used to assess disease risk and guide health management.

## Testing Types

### Carrier Screening

| Disease | Target Population |
|---------|------------------|
| Cystic fibrosis | Couples before pregnancy |
| Spinal muscular atrophy | High-risk populations |
| Thalassemia | High-prevalence areas |

### Genetic Risk Assessment

- Polygenic risk scores
- Family history analysis
- Lifestyle assessment

## Testing Technologies

| Technology | Characteristics |
|------------|-----------------|
| Gene chips | Multi-disease screening |
| Whole exome sequencing | Comprehensive analysis |
| Whole genome sequencing | Most comprehensive |

## Clinical Applications

### Precision Prevention

- Early screening and diagnosis
- Personalized health examinations
- Health management

### Medication Guidance

- Pharmacogenomics
- Efficacy prediction
- Adverse reaction prevention
