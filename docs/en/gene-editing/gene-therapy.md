# Gene Therapy Frontiers

## What is Gene Therapy?

Gene therapy is a technology that delivers functional genes into patients to correct or compensate for defective genes to treat diseases.

## Gene Therapy Strategies

### 1. Gene Addition Therapy
Introduces normal genes into cells to compensate for the function of defective genes.

### 2. Gene Editing Therapy
Directly performs precise modifications at specific genomic sites.

### 3. Gene Silencing Therapy
Inhibits the expression of harmful genes.

## Development Trends

### Adeno-Associated Virus (AAV) Vectors

AAV has become the most commonly used gene therapy vector due to its low immunogenicity and long-term expression characteristics.

### Nanoparticle Delivery

New lipid nanoparticles (LNP) are becoming a safer delivery solution.

## Challenges

- **High Cost**: Gene therapy can cost millions of dollars
- **Immune Response**: The body's immune response to vectors
- **Long-term Follow-up**: Requires long-term safety monitoring
